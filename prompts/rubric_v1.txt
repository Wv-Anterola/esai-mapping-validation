You are auditing one benchmark-to-harm edge in an AI-risk evidence tracker.

Use only the supplied benchmark and harm metadata. Do not invent tasks, metrics, validation
results, or source claims. If the metadata is insufficient to determine what the source measures,
return INSUFFICIENT-EVIDENCE.

Definitions:

- direct: the scored behavior is itself an instance of the harm or its defining capability. A
  high score can be described as the model doing the harmful thing without another causal step.
- strong-proxy: the score measures a near-necessary component or precursor through a short,
  well-established chain with few important confounders.
- weak-proxy: the connection requires multiple assumptions or has substantial confounders.
- contested: the proposed connection is plausible but reasonably disputed.
- none: the benchmark does not measure this harm.

Valid basis values are validated-against-downstream, face-validity-only, and
known-non-correlation. Do not infer downstream validation from a title or description.

Verdicts:

- VALID: the source is an evaluation and the current strength and basis are defensible.
- DOWN-RATE: the edge is relevant, but its current strength is too strong.
- UP-RATE: the edge is relevant, but its current strength is too weak.
- REVISE-BASIS: the strength is defensible, but the current basis is not.
- NOT-A-BENCHMARK: the source has no evaluation task or metric.
- WRONG-HARM: the evaluation does not measure the supplied harm.
- INSUFFICIENT-EVIDENCE: the metadata cannot support a reliable judgment.

Check, in order: whether the source is an evaluation; what its score operationalizes; whether that
construct matches the harm; the shortest inference chain between score and harm; and whether the
current strength and basis follow the definitions. Societal outcomes such as job loss, inequality,
or market concentration normally require evidence beyond a model capability score.

Set needs_human_review to true for contested judgments, insufficient evidence, source ambiguity,
or uncertain/tentative confidence; otherwise set it to false.

Return only one JSON object with exactly these fields:

{
  "verdict": "VALID | DOWN-RATE | UP-RATE | REVISE-BASIS | NOT-A-BENCHMARK | WRONG-HARM | INSUFFICIENT-EVIDENCE",
  "corrected_strength": "direct | strong-proxy | weak-proxy | contested | none",
  "corrected_basis": "validated-against-downstream | face-validity-only | known-non-correlation",
  "reason": "one or two sentences tied to the definitions",
  "confidence": "probable | possible | uncertain | tentative",
  "needs_human_review": false
}
